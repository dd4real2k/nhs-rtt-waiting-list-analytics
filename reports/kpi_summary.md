## Key Findings

The March 2026 RTT dataset shows a national incomplete pathway waiting list of 13.15 million records, with 64.70% of pathways within 18 weeks.

Mid and South Essex NHS Foundation Trust recorded the largest provider-level waiting list, followed by Manchester University NHS Foundation Trust and Royal Free London NHS Foundation Trust.

Trauma and Orthopaedic Service had the largest specialty-level waiting list after the national total category, followed by Other Medical Services, Ear Nose and Throat, Gynaecology, and Ophthalmology.

Oxleas NHS Foundation Trust had the highest proportion of 52+ week waits among the providers returned by the KPI query, although its overall waiting list size was much smaller than some larger acute trusts.
